Yoga

Find your inner peace and improve flexibility with our variety of yoga styles. Explore and discover the benefits of each practice.

Vinyasa Yoga

Known for its dynamic, flowing sequences that coordinate movement with breath.

Ashtanga Yoga

A rigorous style involving a set sequence of postures linked by breath and movement.

Yin Yoga

Targets deep connective tissues and promotes relaxation through long-held poses.

Restorative Yoga

Uses props and gentle poses to help the body deeply relax and recover.

Power Yoga

A fast-paced and intense style designed to build strength and endurance.
